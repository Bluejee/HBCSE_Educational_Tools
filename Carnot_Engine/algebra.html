Algebraic Stage

Here let us figure out the known expressions for the different states of the system.

As we know,
For Isothermal Expansion, Temprature remains a constant.
$ dT = 0 $

For Adiabatic Expansion,
$PV^{\gamma} = Constant$ $\hskip 2em$ where : $P$ - Pressure , $V$ - Volume and $\gamma$ - Ratio of $Cp$ to $Cv$.

For Isothermal Compression, Temprature remains a constant.
$ dT = 0 $

For Adiabatic Compression,
$PV^{\gamma} = Constant$ $\hskip 2em$ where : $P$ - Pressure , $V$ - Volume and $\gamma$ - Ratio of $Cp$ to $Cv$.

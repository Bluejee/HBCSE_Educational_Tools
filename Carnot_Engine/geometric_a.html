Placing the idealized system in a mathematical setting

We place the idealized system in an isolated 2D environment.

The piston is moved from one stand to the other which translates into the motion of the piston.
  • The Piston is initially placed on an isolated stand.
  • Move the Piston to the heat source.
  • Place the piston back on the isolated stand.
  • Move the Piston to the heat sink.
  • Place the piston back on the isolated stand.
